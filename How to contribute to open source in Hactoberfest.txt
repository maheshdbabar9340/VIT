Steps to paticipate into Hactoberfest 

1. Go to Github.com and search for hactoberfest2022
2. Go to projects which are participating into Hactoberfest and take fork to personal github.
3. Make changes to that project and propose those changes with new branch.
4. After that go to upstream account in new tab and make pull request to that project.

This is how you will complete 4 PR and you will be contributed to open source.
